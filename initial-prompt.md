I am in the process of onboarding at ETH Zürich at Gramazio Kohler, I want to create this repo to document my onboarding so that the next person have a better experience. The goal is to write only a README.md that to be used on the next onboard. Here let's assume the person already has access to the two emails addresses, a global one @ethz.ch and one for the department of architecture @arch.ethz.ch and also the two passwords, a global one and one for the network (Wi-Fi/Eduroam). Here are some learnings:

* Both emails point to the same inbox/account
* Email client: You need to use Outlook to be on the same page as the rest of the team. So, go to https://outlook.office.com/mail/ and enter your @ethz.ch
* email, it will redirect to ETH login page.
* Wi-Fi: You will use Eduroam. To connect to it your username will be a third alias that depends on your role. As a PhD student it will be @student-net.ethz.ch instead of just @ethz.ch, so you have to add 'student-net.' to your global email. And your password will be your network password. See more information at https://isg.inf.ethz.ch/Main/ServicesNetworkWireless.
* NAS: There is a NAS at \\\\nas22.ethz.ch\\arch\_ita\_gramazio\_kohler that you can access once you are connected through eduroam. Enter that address in the File Explorer (on Windows). Use your @ethz.ch email as username and your global password. You can also 'Map network drive' to have a quick access to it. "C:\\repos\\github\\daniel-locatelli\\ethz-onboarding\\map-network-drive.jpg". The NAS works both as a storage and as content management system (CMS) for the website https://gramaziokohler.arch.ethz.ch/ (needs more information on how the CMS works).
* Slack: The team will send you an invite to your @arch.ethz.ch
* GitHub: The team will send you an invite to your @arch.ethz.ch
* To access Google Workspace and Microsoft 365 you can access https://unlimited.ethz.ch/en/help and go through their step-by-step:

  * Google: Follow "Google Workspace Getting License" at https://unlimited.ethz.ch/en/help/googlews/getting-started/first-steps. The approval should take 10 minutes, and once approved you will be able to login to Google services using your @ethz.ch email and your global password.
  * Microsoft: Follow "Requesting a M365 License" at https://unlimited.ethz.ch/en/help/m365/getting-started/m365-first-steps. This one should take 5 minutes.
